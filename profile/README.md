## Role Descriptions

Producer - Center of team communication, scheduling, and budgeting the game.
- Game Director - Conveys overall vision of the game. Makes the final decision on choices.
- Creative Director - Responsible for visual identity, style, and aesthetic elements.
    - Art Dirctor - Responsible for working with the Creative Director to create an art style, also creation or delagation of art production.
    - Sound Director - Responsible for working with the Creative Director to create an audio style, also creation or delagation of sound production.
    - Game Designers - Responsible for designing the game's systems, gameplay, and user experience.
    - Level Designers - Designs the spaces, obstacles, and objectives of a video game's world.
    - Story Designers - Responsible for the narrative, character stories, quests, and branching narratives.
    - UX Designers - Design the user interface.
